Hacienda Bodeguera merges Spain’s historic past and stylish present within its four en-suite bedrooms, gated estate. Spanish colonial architecture is represented by ancient stone walls, exposed wood beams, a clay-tiled roof, and the villa’s numerous openings to the beautiful outdoors. On the modern side, Bodeguera uses clean lines, minimalistic decor, and an open concept floor plan to create a space that is both elegant and calming. Outside, Bodeguera is decorated with mature trees, lush greenery, and dynamic views of the distant mountains and valley below.
